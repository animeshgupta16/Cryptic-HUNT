01 · Case Briefing

This isn't a quiz.
It's an investigation.

Every school eventually runs its first cryptic hunt. This is ours. Case Zero opened after SCRATCH TO REVEAL a discrepancy in the archive room surfaced during a routine audit, and nobody has fully explained it since. What we know: the remaining answers are still here, scattered across a place your house already knows well4471 — this campus. What we don't know yet is which house will actually be the one to find them.

There is no traditional scoring. No points added, none deducted, no penalty for a wrong turn or an unlocked hint. Progress is the only currency that matters — every stage your house clears is permanent proof of what you found, and the house that goes furthest in five hours wins. Not the fastest. The furthest.

02 · How It Unfolds

One continuous case.
Not a level-select screen.

Nothing here is numbered “Level 1” or “Puzzle 3.” Every stage is another chapter of the same investigation — each answer hands you evidence, not a password, and every piece of evidence raises a new question on the way to the next one.

03 · Method Of Inquiry

We won't tell you what kind of puzzle you're looking at.

That's not withheld to be difficult — figuring out the method is part of solving it. Evidence will reach you through more than one sense, and more than one layer of the page.

What You See

Images, layouts, timestamps, small inconsistencies. Some details are decoration. Some aren't. The page doesn't tell you which.

What You Hear

Not every clue is meant to be read. Some are meant to be played, slowed down, or listened to twice.

What's Underneath

The structure of a page can hold as much as its content — how it's built, named, and labeled matters exactly as much as what's written on it.
